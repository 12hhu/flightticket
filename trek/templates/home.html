BEST PLACES TO VISIT

Madurai, the cultural capital of Tamil Nadu, is a historic city on the banks of River Vaigai with over 2500 years of documented history. Known as "Thoonga Nagaram," it is the third largest urban agglomeration in Tamil Nadu.

Manali, Himachal Pradesh, is renowned for the historic Hadimba Devi Temple nestled amidst scenic forests, offering a serene atmosphere for visitors to explore its rich history and culture.

Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is celebrated for housing the iconic Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the world, offering a romantic setting for visitors to cherish moments together and capture lasting memories amid its timeless beauty.
